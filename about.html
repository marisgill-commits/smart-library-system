Who We Are

Smart Library is a modern digital library system designed for students and learners. Our goal is to provide easy access to thousands of educational resources, books, and learning materials in one place.

This project is developed as part of DevOps internship training to demonstrate Git workflow, version control and professional frontend development practices.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, easy and digital learning resources for every student.

Our Vision

To become a smart digital library platform for learners worldwide.

Our Goal

To promote knowledge sharing using modern technology.
